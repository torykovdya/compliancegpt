ACME CORPORATION — REMOTE WORK POLICY
Effective Date: January 15, 2026
Owner: Human Resources Department

1. ELIGIBILITY
Employees who have completed their 90-day probation period and whose role does not require physical presence (as determined by their department head) are eligible for remote work.

2. REMOTE WORK DAYS
Eligible employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. In-office days must be coordinated with the team to ensure collaboration and meetings are covered.

3. CORE HOURS
Remote employees must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the company's primary timezone (GMT+3). During this window, employees must respond to messages within 30 minutes.

4. APPROVAL PROCESS
All remote work schedules must be submitted to and approved by the direct manager via the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance. Ad-hoc remote work (due to illness, emergency) does not require prior approval but must be reported on the same day.

5. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
- All employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems, databases, or client data.
- Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all company accounts.
- Sensitive documents must not be stored on personal devices. Use only company-approved cloud storage.
- Screen locks must be enabled with a maximum timeout of 5 minutes.

6. HOME OFFICE STIPEND
Eligible remote employees receive a home office stipend of $500 per calendar year. This can be used for ergonomic furniture, internet upgrades, or office equipment. Receipts must be submitted through the Expense Management system within 30 days of purchase.

7. TAX AND LEGAL
Employees working from abroad for more than 30 consecutive days must notify HR and Finance. Tax implications may apply depending on the host country's regulations. Certain countries are restricted due to data protection laws.

8. EQUIPMENT
Company-issued laptops must be used for all work-related tasks. Personal devices may be used only after approval from IT Security. All company equipment must be returned within 5 business days of employment termination.

9. DATA PROTECTION
Remote employees must comply with the company's Data Classification Policy. Customer data (Level 2 and above) must never be stored, transmitted, or processed on personal devices or unapproved services.

10. VIOLATIONS
Repeated violations of this policy may result in revocation of remote work privileges. Serious security violations (e.g., bypassing VPN, unauthorized data sharing) may lead to disciplinary action up to and including termination.

For questions, contact: hr@acme.internal
Policy review date: July 2026
